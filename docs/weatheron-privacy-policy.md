# Privacy Policy for WeatherOn

**Last Updated:** April 2025

WeatherOn ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the WeatherOn application across all supported platforms (Android, iOS, Desktop, Web, and Server).

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Location Data
To provide accurate weather forecasts, WeatherOn requires access to your location information. This may include:
- **Precise Location:** GPS data from your device (with your explicit permission).
- **Approximate Location:** Derived from your IP address.

### 1.2 Device Information
We may collect information about the device you use to access the app, such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

### 1.3 Usage Data
We may collect information on how the app is accessed and used, including timestamps of access and features interacted with, to help us improve the user experience.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:
- **To provide and maintain the service:** Specifically, to deliver localized weather forecasts, radar data, and geocoding services.
- **To improve our service:** Analyzing usage patterns to enhance app performance and design.
- **To manage your preferences:** Storing your settings locally on your device (e.g., unit preferences, theme selection).

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
WeatherOn stores your preferences and certain cached weather data locally on your device using Jetpack DataStore and SQLDelight. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to our servers unless specifically required for a feature you enable.

### 3.2 Security
The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. However, we may share anonymized or aggregated location data with third-party weather data providers to fetch the meteorological information you request.

## 5. Third-Party Services

WeatherOn uses third-party APIs for weather, geocoding, and radar data. These services may have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
- The right of rectification.
- The right to object.
- The right of restriction.
- The right to data portability.
- The right to withdraw consent.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
